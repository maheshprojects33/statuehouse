Craftsmanship

Our Process

01

Design & Sculpting

Our master sculptors begin with clay models, refining every detail by hand over several days.

02

Mold & Casting

Using the ancient lost-wax (cire perdue) technique, each piece is cast with precision and care.

03

Finishing & Detailing

Hours of hand-chasing, engraving, and polishing bring each statue to life with exquisite detail.

04

Quality & Blessing

Every piece passes rigorous quality checks before receiving a traditional blessing from local monks.
