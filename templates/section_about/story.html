Our Story

Est. 2001 · Lalitpur, Nepal

A Family Legacy of Artisanship

Statue House Pvt. Ltd. was born from a deep love for Nepal's rich sculptural tradition. For over 25 years, our family has worked alongside the finest artisans in the Lalitpur — master craftsmen whose skills have been passed down through generations.

What began as a small workshop in Patan has grown into a trusted name for collectors, galleries, and spiritual seekers worldwide. Yet our mission remains unchanged: to preserve and share the extraordinary beauty of Nepali handicraft with the world.

Every statue that leaves our workshop carries the spirit of the Himalayas — the patience of the mountains, the warmth of our people, and the devotion of our artisans.
